Bansal TMT Loyalty Management App

Designed a loyalty management platform for Bansal Group to drive sales and strengthen relationships with contractors, architects, and engineers. By enabling users to earn and redeem points for purchases made through verified distributors, the platform incentivized repeat business and improved partner retention. The mobile-first, role-based experience simplified ordering and rewards tracking, leading to increased engagement and a noticeable uptick in sales.
